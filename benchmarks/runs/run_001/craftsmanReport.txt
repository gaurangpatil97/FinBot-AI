Critical Findings
1. Severe Routing Failures on Ratio & Concall Sections
Ratio (0% Routing Accuracy): The system failed to route a single question in the Ratio section to the correct Excel collection (craftsman_automation_excel). Instead, it routed to the PDF collection or general collections. Without direct access to the Excel-structured financials, the model could not retrieve key items (like total assets or net profit), resulting in a 0% strict accuracy score.
Concall (15% Routing Accuracy): Concall routing was extremely low. The router consistently failed to search the earnings call transcripts, opting instead for PDF annual reports or Excel structured files, which starved the LLM of the necessary management commentary.
2. Agnostic vs. Company-Specific Performance Discrepancy
Agnostic (7.5% Strict / 22.5% Lenient): Because the agnostic questions were written with generic terms, the RAG system struggled to identify which Craftsman Automation sources were relevant, often failing to route or retrieve correctly. In contrast, Company-Specific questions scored 31.7% Strict / 68.3% Lenient because their specific references to Craftsman entities, plants, and metrics triggered much more precise retrieval.
3. Impact of Newly Added FY22 Q4 Concall Data (crafts22Q4.pdf)
The addition of the FY22 Q4 earnings call transcript successfully resolved several historical data gaps.
Questions CC5 (I&E segment YoY growth comparison), CC7 (Daimler business risk discrepancy), and CX1 (Debt reduction guidance vs actual balance sheet borrowings) succeeded for both Strict and Lenient grading because the system was able to retrieve crafts22Q4.pdf.
Additionally, CC1, CC8, and CC9 succeeded for Lenient grading (achieving partially correct scores) due to elements extracted from the newly added transcript, though they missed some secondary details.
4. Comparison to Astral Ltd Baseline
Overall Lag: Craftsman's overall metrics (33% Routing, 22% Strict, 50% Lenient) lag significantly behind Astral's baseline (58% Routing, 30% Strict, 68% Lenient). This is primarily driven by the catastrophic routing failures in the Ratio and Concall sections, confirming that Craftsman's collection metadata or query expansion mappings require significant optimization compared to the Astral baseline.
