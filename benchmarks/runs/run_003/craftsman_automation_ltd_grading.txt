\[
\text{CAGR}_{\text{Revenue}} = \left(\frac{8069.27}{3182.60}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} - 1 = (2.535)^{0.3333} - 1
\]

Calculate cube root of 2.535: approximately 1.36 (using exact calculator is not possible here, but since 1.36^3 ≈ 2.52, close enough)

\[
\text{CAGR}_{\text{Revenue}} \approx 1.36 - 1 = 0.36 = 36\%
\]

\[
