Question 10 (ID: E10) [Excel]
Q: What is the 3-year CAGR of revenue and the 3-year CAGR of total assets? Is the company growing revenue faster or slower than its asset base is expanding?
Expected Answer: Revenue 3Y CAGR: 8.39, Asset 3Y CAGR: 10.04. Revenue is growing SLOWER than asset base — indicating declining asset utilization efficiency.
Actual Answer:   ## Details

To calculate the 3-year CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) for revenue and total assets, we need the revenue and total assets values for the base year (3 years ago) and the ending year (most recent year).

- Latest year available: FY2026
- Base year for 3-year CAGR: FY2023

### Revenue (Sales) figures from profit_loss sheet:
- FY2023 Sales = 5158.50 Cr
- FY2026 Sales = 6568.60 Cr

### Total Assets figures from balance_sheet sheet:
Total Assets = Sum of all assets components disclosed. The balance sheet provides:
- Net Block
- Capital Work in Progress
- Other Assets
- Receivables
- Inventory
- Cash & Bank

We sum these components for each year to get total assets.

**FY2023 Total Assets:**
- Net Block = 1950.50 Cr
- Capital Work in Progress = 126.10 Cr
- Other Assets = 2285.50 Cr
- Receivables = 354.50 Cr
- Inventory = 874.60 Cr
- Cash & Bank = 682.10 Cr

Sum = 1950.50 + 126.10 + 2285.50 + 354.50 + 874.60 + 682.10 = 6273.30 Cr

**FY2026 Total Assets:**
- Net Block = 2945.00 Cr
- Capital Work in Progress = 88.70 Cr
- Other Assets = 2778.20 Cr
- Receivables = 475.10 Cr
- Inventory = 1117.30 Cr
- Cash & Bank = 943.40 Cr

Sum = 2945.00 + 88.70 + 2778.20 + 475.10 + 1117.30 + 943.40 = 8347.70 Cr

### CAGR Formula:
\[
\text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1
\]
where \( n = 3 \) years.

## Calculation

### Revenue CAGR:
\[
= \left(\frac{6568.60}{5158.50}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} - 1
= (1.273)^{0.3333} - 1
\]

Calculate \( (1.273)^{0.3333} \):

Using a calculator:
\[
(1.273)^{0.3333} \approx 1.083
\]

So,
\[
\text{Revenue CAGR} = 1.083 - 1 = 0.083 = 8.3\%
\]

### Total Assets CAGR:
\[
= \left(\frac{8347.70}{6273.30}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}} - 1
= (1.331)^{0.3333} - 1
\]

Calculate \( (1.331)^{0.3333} \):

Using a calculator:
\[
(1.331)^{0.3333} \approx 1.100
\]

So,
\[
\text{Total Assets CAGR} = 1.100 - 1 = 0.100 = 10.0\%
\]

## Summary

The 3-year CAGR of revenue from FY2023 to FY2026 is approximately 8.3%, while the 3-year CAGR of total assets over the same period is approximately 10.0%. Therefore, the company's asset base is expanding faster than its revenue growth.
