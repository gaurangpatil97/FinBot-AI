Management has reaffirmed the target to double the top line in five years (~15% CAGR) and indicated confidence in achieving or exceeding this growth, with potential for upward revision depending on market conditions.

## Details

- Revenue in FY21: ₹3,176.3 Cr  
- Revenue in FY26: ₹6,568.6 Cr

## Calculation

CAGR formula:  
\[
\text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1
\]

Where:  
- Ending Value = ₹6,568.6 Cr (FY26 revenue)  
- Beginning Value = ₹3,176.3 Cr (FY21 revenue)  
- n = 5 years

Step-by-step:  
\[
\frac{6,568.6}{3,176.3} = 2.067
\]
\[
\text{CAGR} = (2.067)^{\frac{1}{5}} - 1 = 1.163 - 1 = 0.163 \text{ or } 16.3\%
\]

This CAGR of 16.3% exceeds the ~15% CAGR target management set for doubling revenue in 5 years.
