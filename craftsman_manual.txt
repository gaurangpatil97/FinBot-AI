\[

\]

- EBIT = Profit before tax + Interest expense
- Capital Employed = Equity (Share Capital + Reserves) + Borrowings

---

- Capital Employed = Equity + Reserves + Borrowings = 11.93 + 3252.13 + 3623.48 = ₹6887.54 Cr

\[
\text{ROCE}_{2026} = \frac{843.27}{6887.54} \times 100 = 12.24\%
\]

### FY2025 ROCE:

- Capital Employed = Equity + Reserves + Borrowings = 11.93 + 2844.81 + 2358.18 = ₹5214.92 Cr

\[
\text{ROCE}_{2025} = \frac{486.29}{5214.92} \times 100 = 9.33\%
